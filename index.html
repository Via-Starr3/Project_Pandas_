Help pandas!

For a long time pandas were one of endangered species. Let's help them together!

Why is it so important?

Red pandas are becoming fewer and fewer every year. It's people's fault. For a long time now, pandas have been killed by deforestation of the forests that used to be their home. Some are killed by poachers who hunt pandas for their beautiful fur. the extinction of animal species is always bad. So we must protect these cute animals.

How can I help them?

To protect red pandas, all you have to do is follow simple steps:

  • Do not visit zoos with red pandas
  • Do not buy red panda fur products
  • Use less electricity
  • Save paper

Also, you can send a donation to our project or to another foundations
